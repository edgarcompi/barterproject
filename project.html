About The Barter Project

The Barter Project was born in October 2020, as part of an assigment for Entrepreneurial Selling, a course taught by Craig Wortmann at Kellogg School of Management to Full-time MBAs.

I was inspired by the challenge and by the story of One Red Paperclip and why not, this scene of the wolf of Wall Street.

My aspiration is to continue trading items way beyond the length of the course with the ambition to surpass the One Red Paperclip story. It is not about the financial gain, but about proving that there is opportunity for people who want to believe
